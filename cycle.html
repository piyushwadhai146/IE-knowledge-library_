Cycle Time

Cycle time is the total time required to complete one unit of production from the start of the process to the finish. It is an important concept in industrial engineering and production management because it helps measure the efficiency of a production system.

Cycle Time Formula

Cycle Time = Available Production Time / Number of Units Produced

Example

If a factory operates for 480 minutes in one shift and produces 120 units, then:

Cycle Time = 480 / 120 = 4 minutes per unit

This means every product takes approximately 4 minutes to complete.

Benefits of Cycle Time

  • Improves production planning
  • Helps identify bottlenecks
  • Improves process efficiency
  • Helps calculate takt time and line balancing
