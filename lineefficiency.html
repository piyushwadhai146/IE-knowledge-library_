Line Efficiency

Line efficiency measures how effectively a production line operates. Higher efficiency means better utilization of resources.

Formula

Line Efficiency = (Total Work Content / (No. of Workstations × Cycle Time)) × 100

Example

If work content is 200 seconds, cycle time is 50 seconds, and there are 5 stations:

Efficiency = (200 / (5 × 50)) × 100 = 80%
